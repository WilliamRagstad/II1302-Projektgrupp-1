About us

Welcome to AirDash

Our product is a Proof-of-Concept of a smart dashcam - Which can record video and upload the last 30 seconds of video to the cloud in case of an accident. All data saved in the cloud will then be used to generate a heatmap to show which places are more accident-prone. And the video will be retrievable too for any uses.
